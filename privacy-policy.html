Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Abu Ecom ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit abuecom.com or interact with our advertising.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Information you provide when booking a call (name, email address, business details)
  • Usage data collected automatically via cookies and tracking technologies (pages visited, time on site, referral source)
  • Information collected by Meta (Facebook) Pixel about your interactions with our ads and website

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To contact you about your enquiry or booking
  • To measure and improve the performance of our advertising campaigns
  • To understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it
  • To retarget visitors with relevant advertising on Meta platforms (Facebook and Instagram)

3. Meta Pixel

We use the Meta Pixel on this website. This tool allows us to measure the effectiveness of our Facebook and Instagram advertising by understanding actions taken on our site. The Meta Pixel may collect data such as your IP address, browser type, referring page, and pages visited.

Meta's use of this data is governed by Meta's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Meta's data collection via your Facebook ad preferences.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to operate our website and enable advertising features. These include:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use the site
  • Advertising cookies — used by Meta Pixel to deliver and measure ads

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling advertising cookies will not remove ads — it will make them less relevant to you.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc. — for advertising and measurement purposes
  • Cal.com — to process call bookings
  • Service providers who help us operate our business, under confidentiality obligations

6. Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry and maintain our business relationship. You may request deletion at any time.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Contact
