# Abu Ecom

## Product Purpose
Landing page for Abu Ecom, an email marketing agency that helps ecommerce brands turn their email list into their highest-revenue channel. The single goal of this page is to get a qualified ecom brand founder to book a free 30-minute strategy call.

## Register
brand

## Users
Ecommerce brand founders and operators. They run Shopify stores in categories like skincare, supplements, fashion, coffee, pet products, home goods, and beauty. They are busy, skeptical of agencies, have been burned before by overpromising vendors. They care about ROI, not vanity metrics. They need to see proof — real numbers, real clients, real results — before they trust anyone. They are often browsing on mobile.

## Brand
- Name: Abu Ecom
- Tone: Direct, confident, no fluff. We know what we're doing and we don't need to oversell it. Short sentences. Real numbers. Proof over claims.
- Anti-references: generic SaaS landing pages, flashy agency websites with stock photos, anything with gradient text or glassmorphism
- The design should feel like baked.design — dark, editorial, typographically bold, mobile-first, premium but approachable

## Strategic Principles
1. Every section should push toward one action: booking the strategy call
2. Social proof (testimonials + results) should be interleaved with content, not siloed
3. Mobile layout comes first — most visitors will be on iPhone
4. Numbers build trust faster than words
5. The page should feel fast and load instantly — no heavy assets

## Key Differentiators
- Email-only focus (not a full-service agency)
- Klaviyo specialists
- Results in 60 days or less
- Real Klaviyo screenshots as proof
- Founder-accessible pricing

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- No stock photos
- No gradient text
- No hero metric template (big number + small label grid on hero)
- No modal pop-ups
- No identical card grids
- No em dashes in copy
